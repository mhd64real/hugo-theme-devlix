Devlix gives you a profile card, a paginated blog, an ASCII-art post header, and shortcodes, all from plain markdown and config.

## A captioned figure

Use the `figure` shortcode for centered images with captions:

{{< figure src="https://picsum.photos/seed/figure/700/400" caption="A centered, captioned figure." alt="Example" width="70%" >}}

## Images never crop

Drop any image into a post and it scales to fit, centered, with a thin border. No cropping, no overflow.

![A sample image](https://picsum.photos/seed/inline/900/500)

That is all it takes.
